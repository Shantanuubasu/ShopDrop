Welcome to ShopDrop!

ShopDrop Private Limited is an Indian e-commerce company headquartered in Mumbai, officially established as a private limited company in 2023. Initially specializing in online electronics sales, the company later diversified its offerings to include various product categories such as fashion, home essentials, groceries, and lifestyle products. In the highly competitive Indian e-commerce landscape, ShopDrop faces challenges from both international and domestic rivals. Presently, as of January 2024, ShopDrop commands a substantial 29.5% market share in the Indian e-commerce industry. Notably, the company has achieved dominance in the electronics segment, owing to strategic acquisitions like De-vice.com. In the realm of electronics and mobile phones, ShopDrop is recognized as a formidable player, often being described as closely competitive with other major industry players.
